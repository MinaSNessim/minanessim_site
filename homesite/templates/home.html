About

Back in the day, I was mesmerized by the mystery of solving The Rubik’s cube. One YouTube took me on a journey where something that seemed impossible became a puzzle I managed to solve.

That made me realize, that every obstacle, every business challenge, is a puzzle waiting to be solved. And just like a Rubik’s cube, it only takes the right algorithm to find the solution.

Today, I'm a dedicated data scientist and machine learning analyst, skilled in Python, SQL, VBA, and more. Exploring the mysteries hidden within raw data, I transform it into actionable insights, fostering heightened business efficiency.

I'm here to help your business thrive by leveraging data to its fullest potential.
