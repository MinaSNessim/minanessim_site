About Me

Greetings, I'm Mina. In my early days, I found myself entranced with the mysteries of the Rubik’s Cube. One day I found a YouTube channel talking about this puzzle, and just in few days I was able to solve it.

This revelation dawned on me: every problem, every business challenge, mirrors a puzzle awaiting a solution. Much like a Rubik’s Cube, it merely requires the right algorithm to unveil the answers.

Today, delving into the realm of data science, I collaborate with businesses to enhance their operations, elevate efficiency, and optimize profits. My experience includes impactful projects with Fortune 500 companies, such as reducing shipment lead times from 90 days to a swift 40 days by recalibrating routes and hubs.

Beyond data, I find joy in strategic gaming, with Yu-Gi-Oh ranking high among my favorites. This complicated game, unlike chess, remains a captivating challenge where AI has yet to consistently outmatch human prowess, at least that still gives us hope in human intelligence, right?

Let's keep working together to solve tough problems and turn challenges into successes.
