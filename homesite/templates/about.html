About the Founder

Greetings, I'm Mina. In my early days, I found enchantment in unraveling the mysteries of the Rubik’s Cube. A serendipitous YouTube journey turned the seemingly impossible into a puzzle I triumphantly solved. This revelation dawned on me: every hurdle, every business challenge, mirrors a puzzle awaiting a solution. Much like a Rubik’s Cube, it merely requires the right algorithm to unveil the answers. Let's continue this journey of unraveling complexities and transforming challenges into triumphs.
